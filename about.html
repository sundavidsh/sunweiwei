About me

A proactive multilingual technical manager with solid EE technical background in international certification/approval and market access area.

David studied Electrical Engineering and Automation and received his bachelor degree of engineering from the Huazhong University of Science & Technology in 2007. David is currently pursuing his MBA programme at the Shanghai International Studies University.

He joined SGS in 2010 and was assigned to lead the Small Household Appliance Shanghai team in 2011 and established Suzhou HOUS EE performance and ErP lab in 2014.
He was approved and promoted as SGS Global reviewer and Supervisor/Manager of the Shanghai and Suzhou team for lab operation and internal audit, technical review, training/seminar,technical support and execute business development and marketing strategy.

He actively participated in several technical committees drafting HOUS standards and his research interests lie in household appliances regulations and standards.

He can speak Chinese, English, French, Japanese and German.
He is familiar with HTML, CSS+JSP, Windows Server, SPSS etc.
